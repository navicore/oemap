PRIVACY POLICY

Welcome to OeMap (the "Site").

We understand that privacy online is important to users of our Site. This statement governs our privacy policies with respect to those users of our mobile software who post information to the site.

"Personally Identifiable Information"
refers to any information that identifies or can be used to identify, contact, or locate the person to whom such information pertains, including, but not limited to, name, address, phone number, fax number, email address, financial profiles, social security number, and credit card information. Personally Identifiable Information does not include information that is collected anonymously (that is, without identification of the individual user)
or demographic information not connected to an identified individual.

What Personally Identifiable Information is collected?
We may collect the user profile "Dispaly Name" information from all of our mobile software users.

How does the Site use Personally Identifiable Information?
We will only use the collected "Display Name" in the context of the user explicitly sharing that information. We will not use their information for any other purpose.

With whom may the information may be shared?
Your "Display Name" is shared with other users on the OeMap system who have joined the same sharable space as you have. If you share your info to "My San Fransisco Photowalk Map" then only users who have joined that map can see your information.

How is Personally Identifiable Information stored?
Personally Identifiable Information collected by OeMap is securely stored and is not accessible to third parties.

What choices are available to Visitors regarding collection, use and distribution of the information?
Using the software settings, a mobile software user may change the name displayed to other users without restriction. That new name will be used and we will not store the real "Display Name" anywhere.

Are Cookies Used on the Site?
We use Cookies to enable the "not resharable" feature in the mobile software, ensuring only users you have allowed to join your map space may join your map space.

How does OeMap use login information?
There is no OeMap login!

Can a Visitor delete or deactivate Personally Identifiable Information collected by the Site?
Yes, select the "Quit Map" menu option.

What happens if the Privacy Policy Changes?
We will let our Visitors and Authorized Customers know about changes to our privacy policy by posting such changes on the Site. However, if we are changing our privacy policy in a manner that might cause disclosure of Personally Identifiable Information that a Visitor or Authorized Customer has previously requested not be disclosed, we will announce to our mobile users with a software update push message to allow such Visitor or Authorized Customer to prevent such disclosure.

Links:
This web site contains links to other web sites. Please note that when you click on one of these links, you are moving to another web site. We encourage you to read the privacy statements of these linked sites as their privacy policies may differ from ours.
